OUR VISION

To become a 21st century World Class Sport Institute through





Sports Science

Optimise athletes' performances through leading-edge science, performance testing and monitoring, applied research and education.

Sports Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of injuries associated with athletic training and participation to sustain their athletic careers.

Sports Technology

Promoting the application of science and technology using R&D in sports to provide athletes with the edge for success.








What is SSI?

SSI endeavors to be a sport institute of significance where we provide the best support to Team Singapore athletes, so that they can perform at their maximum potential, to fulfill their sporting aspirations and to inspire the Singapore Spirit.

OUR FACILITIES



GYM

Packed with state-of-the-art equipments that cater to athletes of all sports.
Provides TeamSG athletes with experienced personal trainers that manages the team by planning and supervising their strength and conditioning trainings.






Physiotherapy

Injuries are inevitable in a professional athletes career. SSI provides qualified physiotherapists that arrange the necessary care and procedures for injury recovery to TeamSG athletes to ensure that athletes can return to their normal conditions after injury.






Testing

Advanced technology that tracks performance to allow athletes and trainers to evaluate training effectiveness and reassess training methods to develop athletes for podium success.








Nutrition

Nutritionists that educate athletes about sports nutrition, providing athletes one more way to enhance their performance further. Teaching athletes how to use food to refuel, hydrate, and recover their bodies in conjunction with their training.
